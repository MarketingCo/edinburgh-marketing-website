The Rolling Pin was a local favorite in Leith but struggled to attract customers from other parts of Edinburgh. 

Through our comprehensive local SEO strategy, we optimized their Google Business Profile and created a series of neighborhood-targeted ad campaigns. 

Within 6 months, they opened their second location in the New Town.
