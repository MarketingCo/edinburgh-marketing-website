Ranking in Edinburgh requires a local-first approach. Here are our top 5 tips:

1. **Claim Your Google Business Profile:** Ensure your address and phone number match across the web.
2. **Use Local Edinburgh Keywords:** Mention specific neighborhoods like Leith, Stockbridge, and New Town.
3. **Get Local Backlinks:** Connect with local Scottish directories and news sites.
4. **Encourage Reviews:** Ask your loyal Edinburgh customers to leave reviews on Google.
5. **Optimize for Mobile:** Most local searches happen on the move.

By focusing on these strategies, your business will stand out in the crowded Edinburgh market.
